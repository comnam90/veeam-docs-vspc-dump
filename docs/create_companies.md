# Creating Companies


To create a new company in Veeam Service Provider Console, perform the following steps:

1. [Launch the New Company wizard](launch_tenant_wizard.md).
2. [Specify company details](specify_company_details.md).
3. [Specify user credentials](specify_user_credentials.md).
4. [Configure services](configure_company_services.md).
5. [Choose a subscription plan](choose_subscription.md).
6. [Configure multi-factor authentication](company_mfa.md).
7. [Configure notification settings](company_alarms.md).
8. [Review company settings](review_tenant_settings.md).
9. [Create company locations](create_tenant_locations.md).
