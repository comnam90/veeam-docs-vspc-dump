# Veeam Service Provider Console Management Capabilities


The following table provides overview on Veeam Service Provider Console features available for the latest versions of managed products.

Veeam Service Provider Console Management Capabilities

| Functionality | Veeam Backup & Replication | Veeam backup agents Managed by Backup Server | Veeam backup agents Managed by Console | Veeam ONE | Veeam Backup for Microsoft 365 | Veeam Backup for AWS/Microsoft Azure/Google Cloud Managed by Veeam Cloud Connect | Veeam Backup for AWS/Microsoft Azure/Google Cloud Manged by Veeam Backup & Replication |
| Deploy using Veeam Service Provider Console | Full | N/A | Full | N/A | N/A | Full | N/A |
| Update using Veeam Service Provider Console | Full | N/A | Full | N/A | N/A | Full | N/A |
| Patch using Veeam Service Provider Console | Full | N/A | Full | N/A | N/A | Full | N/A |
| Job creation | Hosted jobs only | N/A | Full | N/A | Full | Full | N/A |
| Job management | Full (job editing is available for hosted jobs only) | Full (except for job editing) | Full | N/A | Full | Full | Full |
| Restore | N/A | N/A | Full | N/A | Full | Full | N/A |
| Viewing and exporting data | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full |
| Logging | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | N/A | N/A |
| Licensing | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full |
| Update notifications | Full | N/A | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full |
| Billing | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full |
| Backup reports | Full | Full | Full | N/A | Full | Full | Full |
| Alarms | Full | Full | Full | Full1 | Full | Full | Full |
| REST API | Full | Full | Full | Full (except for alarms triggered on Veeam ONE servers) | Full | Full2 | Full2 |

1 Veeam Service Provider Console also supports monitoring and management of alarms triggered on Veeam ONE servers.

2 For Veeam Backup for Google Cloud, some REST API methods are in the Preview mode.
