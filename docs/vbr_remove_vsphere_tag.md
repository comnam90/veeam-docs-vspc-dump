# Removing Tags from Companies


If you want to change a VMware vSphere tag for a company, you must first remove the previously assigned tag.

To remove a VMware vSphere tag from a client company:

1. Log in to Veeam Service Provider Console.

For details, see [Accessing Veeam Service Provider Console](access_vac.md).

1. At the top right corner of the Veeam Service Provider Console window, click Configuration.
2. In the configuration menu on the left, click Catalog.
3. Click the Veeam Backup & Replication plugin tile.
4. In the menu on the left, click Resources.
5. Select the necessary company in the list.
6. At the top of the list, click Remove vSphere Tag.
7. In the confirmation window, click Yes.

After you remove a VMware vSphere tag from a company, you will be able to perform all management tasks for jobs created for this company except for job editing.
