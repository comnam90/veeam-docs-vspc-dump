# Step 1. Launch New Backup Policy Wizard


To launch the New Mac Backup Policy wizard:

1. Log in to Veeam Service Provider Console.

For details, see [Accessing Veeam Service Provider Console](access_vac.md).

1. At the top right corner of the Veeam Service Provider Console window, click Configuration.
2. In the configuration menu on the left, click Templates.
3. Navigate to the Backup Policies tab.
4. At the top of the list, click New and select Mac.

Alternatively, you can right-click the backup policies list, choose New and select Mac.

Veeam Service Provider Console will launch the Create New Mac Backup Policy wizard.
