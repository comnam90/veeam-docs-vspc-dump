# Managing Veeam Backup & Replication


Veeam Service Provider Console allows you to manage Veeam Backup & Replication servers on behalf of companies that act as consumers of backup services. In the Client Portal, you can manage VM and file protection jobs and run failover plans configured on managed Veeam Backup & Replication servers, as well as monitor and manage Veeam Backup & Replication infrastructures.

To manage Veeam Backup & Replication servers, you must complete the following steps:

1. [Connect Veeam Backup & Replication servers](connect_backup_servers.md).

To manage Veeam Backup & Replication servers, you must first connect them to Veeam Service Provider Console.

1. [Manage Veeam Backup & Replication jobs](manage_backup_jobs.md).

To administer data protection operations in managed virtual infrastructures, you can start, stop, enable and disable jobs configured on managed Veeam Backup & Replication servers.

1. [Manage failover plans](manage_failover_plans.md).

To prevent data loss in case of an accident or a major failure, you can manage failover plans configured on managed Veeam Backup & Replication servers.
