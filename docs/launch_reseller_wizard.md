# Step 1. Launch New Reseller Wizard


To launch the New Reseller wizard:

1. Log in to Veeam Service Provider Console.

For details, see [Accessing Veeam Service Provider Console](access_vac.md).

1. In the menu on the left, click Resellers.
2. At the top of the list, click New.

Alternatively, you can right-click the reseller list and choose New.
